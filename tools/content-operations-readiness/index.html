    Method + privacy

    A directional baseline, not a benchmark.

    The snapshot calculates only from your entries. It does not forecast revenue, headcount, realized savings, or technology ROI.

    Read the field guide
    What the snapshot calculates

    Approved output equals attempts multiplied by final approval rate. Review share equals review and correction hours divided by total modeled labor hours. Cost per approved output appears only when labor cost is entered and approved output is above zero.

    How recommendation rules work

    Control gaps take priority. With all four controls confirmed, approval below 80% routes to approval-loss work; approval at or above 80% with review/correction share at or above 25% can support one controlled AI-assisted test. These are transparent interface rules, not industry benchmarks.

    Privacy and limitations

    Inputs and results stay in this browser. No analytics, storage, network submission, AI service, form endpoint, or personalized result URL is used on this page. Missing values are not treated as zero. Zero approved output returns an explicit not-calculable state.
