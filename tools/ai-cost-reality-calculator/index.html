Definition and methodology

How to calculate AI cost per successful outcome

AI activity is not the same as a usable business result. This calculator estimates annualized unit economics after accounting for the share of AI-assisted attempts expected to meet the required standard.

Total annual AI-assisted cost÷Successful AI-assisted outcomes per year= AI cost per successful outcome
What the five inputs represent

Enter current successful outcomes per year, current annual process cost, expected AI-assisted attempts per year, expected success rate, and total annual AI-assisted cost. A successful outcome is a finished business result that meets the required standard. Use the same standard for the current and AI-assisted scenarios.

What the estimate returns

The result shows current cost per successful outcome, AI cost per attempt, AI cost per successful outcome, expected failed attempts, estimated cost allocated to failed work, and a directional current-versus-AI cost signal.

How failed AI attempts are treated

Failed attempts are estimated from the expected success rate. Annual AI-assisted cost is spread across all attempts, which makes the cost associated with failed, corrected, or unusable work visible.

Privacy and limitations

Inputs stay in the browser and are not submitted. Formula 1.1.0 is a directional planning model, not accounting, legal, investment, or workforce advice. Validate assumptions, evidence quality, and implementation costs before making a decision.
