## Claim-boundary checks

- Klarna’s savings and assistant figures remain company-reported.
- Air Canada is limited to the British Columbia Civil Resolution Tribunal decision.
- The Uber transcript retrieval limitation remains visible in the web edition.
- Hypothetical examples and future-looking measures are not presented as achieved results.
- The PDF is not regenerated or destructively patched; its approved authorship and AI-assistance disclosure are preserved from the authoritative final package.
