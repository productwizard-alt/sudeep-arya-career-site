Executive summary

A subscription workflow built for enrollment, payment support, and operating execution.

Banfield Mexico needed a cleaner wellness plan subscription workflow that could support customer enrollment, recurring subscription management, and in-person commerce activity without creating unnecessary operating friction.

The project connected Shopify Commerce, Loop Subscriptions, Shopify POS terminal workflows, payment workflow improvements, and renewal/payment notification support into a launchable operating model.

The work required coordination across Banfield, Mars/MGS Digital, and implementation partners so business requirements, platform workflow, subscription logic, and go-live execution moved together.

Business problem

Wellness-plan commerce needed a cleaner operating workflow.

  • Wellness-plan enrollment and subscription management needed a cleaner operating workflow.
  • In-person and recurring payment workflows needed better support.
  • Customer enrollment and renewal communication needed more structured data capture.
  • Manual subscription management created operating friction.
