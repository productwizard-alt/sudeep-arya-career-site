Conversation topics

Talk titles with enough friction to be useful.

The strongest conversations usually start with a real business question: where growth breaks, why teams get stuck, and what leaders need to see before they spend, migrate, automate, or reorganize.

Marketplaces and Amazon

  • Amazon 1P vs. 3P: Where Growth, Margin, and Control Really BreakHow brands should think about channel structure, ownership, tradeoffs, and operational consequences.
  • Marketplace Growth Without Operational Discipline Does Not ScaleWhy catalog quality, inventory, pricing, media, fulfillment, and governance matter as much as demand.

DTC and Shopify+

  • Shopify+ Beyond Replatforming: Building a DTC Business That Actually Operates BetterA platform move only matters if it improves speed, merchandising, measurement, and team workflows.
  • The PDP Is Still the StorefrontHow product content, proof, images, reviews, pricing, and fulfillment signals shape conversion.
  • Subscription Commerce Beyond the Sign-Up ButtonWhy recurring revenue depends on payments, support, retention, timing, and customer trust.

Retail media and measurement

  • Retail Media Without Accountability Is Just SpendHow to connect media investment to margin, inventory, incrementality, and executive decision-making.
  • Connecting Media, Merchandising, Inventory, and ProfitabilityCommerce performance gets clearer when teams stop treating each lever as a separate report.
  • What Executives Actually Need From Commerce AnalyticsDashboards should help leaders decide, not just prove that activity happened.

AI and commerce technology

  • AI in Ecommerce: What Is Useful, What Is Theater, and What Leaders Keep Getting WrongA practical look at where AI helps content, testing, workflows, and decision support.
  • Ecommerce Transformation Without Breaking the BusinessHow to modernize platforms and processes while the daily revenue engine still has to run.

Ecommerce operations and leadership

  • Why Ecommerce Teams Get Trapped in Reactive WorkThe operating patterns that keep teams chasing issues instead of building durable growth systems.
  • Building Better Ecommerce With Smaller Budgets and Clearer PrioritiesHow focus, ownership, and sequencing can outperform another scattered list of initiatives.

What I bring to the conversation

I speak from the operating seat.

The useful part is not another trend forecast. It is an honest discussion of what happens when strategy meets inventory, margin, media, platforms, teams, and customers.

Best for audiences that want clear examples, honest tradeoffs, and a direct link between strategy and execution.

AI does not fix a broken operating model.

Retail media without margin visibility is incomplete.

A platform migration is not a growth strategy.

Most dashboards show activity. Fewer explain what to do next.
