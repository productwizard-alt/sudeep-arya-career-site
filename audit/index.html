Audit FAQ

Questions the audit is built to answer.

What is included in the Digital Acceleration Audit?

The audit reviews the most relevant growth blockers across Amazon, DTC, Shopify, marketplaces, retail media, PDP conversion, SEO, merchandising, analytics, and commerce operations.

Who is the audit designed for?

It is designed for DTC brands, Amazon marketplace brands, consumer product teams, founders, ecommerce operators, agencies, and hiring teams who want to see how Sudeep thinks through digital growth problems.

What happens after the session?

The session should leave the team with a clearer diagnosis of the biggest blockers, a prioritized set of fixes, and direction on what to address first.

Is the audit intended for hiring teams?

Hiring teams can use the audit to understand Sudeep's operating approach, but role-specific conversations should start from the career profile or contact page.
